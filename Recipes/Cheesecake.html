Easy No Bake CheeseCake

Ingredients Required

Cheesecake Crust

    225g of graham crackers (about 14 crackers) 85g unsalted butter, melted

Cheesecake Fiiling

    1 tablespoon unflavoured gelatin 1/4 cup cold water 450g cream cheese, room temperature 1 1/2 (175g) powdered sugar 2 teaspoons vanilla extract 1 vanilla bean 2 cups cold heavy cream

Stawberry sauce (optional)

    450g fresh or frozen sliced strawberries 2/3 cups (135g) granulated white sugar 2 teaspoons cornstarch 2 teaspoons cold water 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar, optional
